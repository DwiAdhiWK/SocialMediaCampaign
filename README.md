# Project Title
Social Media Funnel Analysis on Popular Platform for Effective Campaign

## Repository Outline
Dashboard.pbix - Dashboard for the analysis
README.md - Description of the Project
social_media_campaign_db.csv - dataset

## Business Problem
The objective of this dashboard is to conduct a funnel analysis of popular platform such as Ticktok, Instagram, and Facebook. The analysis explore the conversion rate of various campaign on each platform such that maximizing clicks while minimizing the cost of campaign can be reach.

## Key Feature
- Impression and Reach comparison
- Engagement and Clicks comparison
- Campaign per Platform
- Conversion Rates table
- Campaign Budget table

## Key Business Questions Answered
- How does Impression and Reach compare for each campaign?
- How does Engagement and Clicks compare for each campaign?
- How many campaign conducted on each platform?
- What is the conversion rate of each campaign?
- How much cost does a campaign need to generate one click?

## Insight and Discoveries
- Product Launch has the highest number Impression (1057K) and Reach (535K)
- Product Launch has the highest number of Engagement(54383) and Awareness has the highest number of Clicks (7856)
- Tiktok is mainly used for Product Launch (7), Instagram for Promo Month (6), and Facebook has equally similar count of campaign.
- Facebook engagement rate is around 0.08% with Brand Collaboration with the highest rate (0.10%), Instagram at 0.07% with Brand Collaboration at (0.11%), and Tiktok at  0.06% with Promo Month the highest (0.08%)
- The average cost per click of Facebook is $0.4496 with Awerness as the cheapest $0.2645, Instagram at $0.3374 with Awareness at $0.1173 and Tiktok at $0.2937 with Awareness as the cheapest $0.2343.
